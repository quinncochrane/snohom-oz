About Me

All about how I ended up where I am now.

  • 1997-2000

    My Humble Beginnings

    I worked for 3 years at Boeing, starting out picking/counting parts. I wanted to do something more challenging so I transfered to the Purchasing department and became an Supply Chain Management Analyst. Here I took advantage of a lot of the free courses Boeing offers, and I learned VB and VBA. I applied what I learned to build an app that allowed union/non-union employees to track shortages.

    Sadly, Y2K happened and we all died....

  • 2000-2012

    ...Actually, I started working at Philips Healthcare

    I worked at Philips for 11 years because it was a great company with a solid growth path. I started in Material Planning and ultimately transitioned to Supply Chain Project Manager. As my focus was always in new product innovation, I had the privaledge to work with some of the worlds best and brightest engineers to create industry leading and life saving Ultrasound devices. In my time I managed OEM component lifecylces, drove vendor selection and make/buy analysis, ensured supply chain readiness for releases and lead manufacturing location strategy. In my time at Philips I also completed my Bachelors in Management and Operations from WSU (Go Cougs!)

  • 12 years later...

    Sterling Talent Solutions

    I went on to work as Director of Product, Platform at Sterling Talent Solutions, a SaaS cloud solution for background screening and onboarding that is used by over 25K cusotmers and 25% of fortune 500 customers. When I came to Sterling Talent Solutions I was head of the Professional Services department, which gave me an opportunity to be customer facing. I transitioned to Product a couple years later to develop a set of self-service configuration tools. I like the opprotunities that I have to develop highly technical front/back-end solutions, but it also rewarding to be a part of a company that children, employees, and employers safter.

  • Amazon

    Most recently

    Alexa Skills Development

    Since 2019 I've been able to apply my skillset at Amazon as a Sr. Technical Program Manager delivering technology that developers use to make thousands of skills for Alexa.

  • Balance
