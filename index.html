About Trevor Project

We're highlighting the Trevor Project as an organization that is helping teens in crisis.

  • Trevor Project

    https://www.thetrevorproject.org/

    The Trevor Project wants to ensure there is a welcoming and loving world for everyone. Teens can easily find a counselor if they are struggling in that moment, or information to help someone else.

  • LGTBQ+ Youth

    Provide information and support to LGBTQ+ 24/7 all year round.

    Trevor project will provide extensive training to those that want to become crisis councelors for youth, so that they can have someone to talk to in times when they feel like giving up. Youth can meet friends and allies in the Trevor Space.

  • No thanks

    We get it, you might want to support children in other ways.

    While we have very personal reasons that Trevor Project is ideal organization to raise money for, we understand there is a rainbow of perspectives and ways to support children. We hope that you will consider donating to the Childrens' charity of your choice if you are moved to and got some joy out of this display.
